About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Vanessa Prema. I’m a full stack software developer with passions for design, science, and innovation. My background in materials engineering landed me an internship at a fashion technology incubator in Berlin, Germany and that is where my love for code was born. I collaborated with designers to create masterpieces that integrated micro-controllers, alternative textiles, and 3D printing.

Months later, I began working as a project engineer designing machine parts for a packaging firm. While it helped me refine my 3D modeling and drafting skills, I found myself drawn back to programming. I began developing an Excel macro to decrease product release time, and realized that my passion lies in software development. Now after graduating from the Flatiron School bootcamp, I hope to take my life experiences and use it to help other companies develop tools to optimize their business processes.
